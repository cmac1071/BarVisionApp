What BarVision Does

01

Shelf Scan

Point your camera at your bar. BarVision identifies every bottle — brand, spirit category, fill level — in seconds. No typing. No searching.

02

Make Now

Instantly see every cocktail you can make with exactly what you have. Filtered, ranked, and ready to pour.

03

One Bottle Away

See which recipes you're missing just one ingredient for. Know exactly what to buy to unlock the most new drinks.

04

Guided Make Mode

Step-by-step instructions with technique cues, timers, and scaled measurements. From novice to confident home bartender.

05

Smart Substitutions

Missing an ingredient? BarVision reasons about your shelf and suggests the best substitute, so nothing stops the pour.

06

Gap Analysis

A ranked list of the single bottles that would unlock the most new recipes. The smartest shopping list your bar has ever had.

How It Works

1

Scan Your Shelf

Open BarVision and point your iPhone at your spirits collection. Tap once to capture, or use pan mode to slowly sweep across a wide shelf. The AI reads every label.

Computer Vision · GPT-4o
2

Confirm Your Inventory

Review the detected bottles, correct anything the camera missed, and mark fill levels. Your inventory lives privately on your device — no cloud storage of your data.

On-Device Storage · Private by Design
3

See What You Can Make

Instantly browse the full list of cocktails you can make right now, what you're one bottle away from, and smart suggestions for what to buy next.

600+ Recipes · Offline Matching
4

Pour With Confidence

Tap any recipe for guided make mode. Follow each step with technique cues, built-in timers, and measurements that scale to your serving size.

Step-by-Step · Batch Scaling
